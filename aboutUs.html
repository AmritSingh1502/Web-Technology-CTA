About Us

SDMCET QUERY is a platform created for the SDMCET community to ask questions and get answers. Our mission is to foster a collaborative learning environment where everyone can share their knowledge and learn from others.

We believe that everyone has something valuable to contribute, and we encourage all members of the SDMCET community to participate. Whether you have a question about coursework, campus life, or anything else related to SDMCET, don't hesitate to ask!
